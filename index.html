AMRAP 5:00 (or more as needed)

2. Wrist Stretch — 5-10 seconds each hand. Place hands flat on the wall and apply light pressure.
3. Wrist Circles — 5 circles in each direction per arm.
4. Bodyweight Calf Raises — 10 reps. Slow and controlled.
5. Plate Hops — 10 hops. Two-foot hop over a plate, focus on consistent landing mechanics — ball of foot, soft knees, knees together.
6. Single Under / Double Under Practice — 30 seconds. Use whatever you're working on.
Optional but recommended before every session. Takes about 5 minutes and primes the movement patterns you are about to train.

2. Hollow Rocks — 5 reps. Lumbar flat, shape never breaks.
3. Supermans — 5 reps. Face down, arms overhead, lift chest and legs simultaneously. Hold briefly at the top.

Optional HSPU Warm-Up
~3 min · Do this before every HSPU session
▾
// The Warm-Up
AMRAP 5:00 (or more as needed)
1. Wrist/Forearm Stretch — 5-10 seconds. Place hands flat on the wall and apply light pressure to stretch wrists and forearms.
2. Wrist Circles — 5 circles in each direction per arm.
3. Scap Push-Ups — 5 reps. From a push-up position, protract and retract the shoulder blades. No arm bend.
4. Pike Push-Ups — 5 reps. Hips high, head to floor, full range of motion.
5. Handstand Shoulder Taps — 10 reps. Hands on floor, feet on wall. Tap each shoulder alternately while maintaining a tight body position.
This warm-up is optional but recommended before every session. It takes 3 minutes and makes every rep safer and more productive.
