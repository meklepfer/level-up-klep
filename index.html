// The Warm-Up
1. Wall Shoulder Taps — 3×10 each side. Hands on floor, feet on wall, slow and controlled.
2. Pike Push-Up — 2×8 slow. Full range, head to floor. This is your movement warm-up.
3. Scap Push-Up — 2×10. Protract and retract the shoulder blades at the top of a push-up position. No arm bend.
4. Wrist Prep — Circles each direction 15 reps, then loaded wrist stretch 30 seconds each hand.
This warm-up is optional but recommended before every session. It takes 3 minutes and makes every rep safer and more productive.
